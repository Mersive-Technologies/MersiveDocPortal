Step 8: Validate Your Deployment

You can validate the functionality of a Solstice deployment with the following steps.

Step 1: Connect devices to network. Connect a user device such as a Windows or Mac laptop or an Apple or Android mobile device to a network attached to the Solstice Pod display.
   
Step 2: Download the Solstice app. Open a web browser and enter the IP address shown on the welcome screen of the Solstice Pod display. Follow the link to get the Solstice app.
   
Step 3: Verify available devices are showing. Launch the Solstice app. The discovery panel should appear with a list of Solstice displays available for connection. If the discovery panel is empty, you may still need to configure SDS.
   
Step 4: Connect to the Solstice Pod display. Click or tap the name of the desired Solstice Pod display to connect the user's device to that display.
   
Step 5: Test sharing options. While connected to a Solstice display, test the following sharing options with your Solstice app. If playing a video, you should see about 22-30 fps from a 1080p resolution device, depending on its specs. Audio should be synchronized.
  • Share desktop, or mirror mobile device screen (AirPlay or Android screen mirroring)
  • Share application window (such as PowerPoint or a PDF)
  • Share media files (video files and images)
