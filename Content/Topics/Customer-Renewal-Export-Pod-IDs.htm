How to Export Pod IDs for Subscription Renewal

Whether you use Solstice Cloud or Solstice Dashboard to manage your organization's Solstice Pods, you can export the IDs of all the Pods you manage to

How to View Export Pod IDs to a CSV File

In Solstice Cloud:

  1. Log in to https://cloud.mersive.com. In the left sidebar, expand the Monitor section and select Deployment.

     

     

  2. Beneath the Pod information listed in the Deployment table, click Export to CSV.

     

    You can click the Status column header to sort the Pods with lapsed subscription periods together at the top of the Deployment table before exporting the CSV file.

  3. Save the CSV file containing your Pod IDs, then send it as an attachment to to your account manager at renew@mersive.com.

     

     

In Solstice Dashboard:

  1. Locate the Solstice Instances on the left side of the Dashboard

     

     

  2. Right click any of the Pods and select Export all displays to file from the menu.

     

    If your deployment contains Pods that are of different hardware generations or running different versions of the Solstice software, you should export all displays for each grouping that appears in Your Solstice Instances.

  3. Save the CSV file containing your Pod IDs, then send it as an attachment to your account manager at renew@mersive.com.
