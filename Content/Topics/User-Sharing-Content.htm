Sharing Content to the Solstice Display

Users can share and control content on the Solstice display using the Solstice app on their laptop or mobile device. Also see Mirroring Your Device to a Solstice Display to learn about using Windows Miracast or Apple Airplay to mirror a device screen to the display without needing to download the Solstice app.
