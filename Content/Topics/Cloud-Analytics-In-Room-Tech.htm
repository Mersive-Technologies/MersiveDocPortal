Solstice Cloud Analytics: In-Room Tech

The In-Room Tech analytics page in Solstice Cloud allows you see how teams collaborate when they come together in meeting and learning spaces by providing data on the types of devices that are connecting to your organization's Solstice Pods, and the methods that are being used to share content. For Pods used to support video conferencing, you can display data about the number of meetings that use conferencing (i.e. hybrid meetings) as well as the conferencing platforms used to share to Solstice (e.g. Zoom, Teams, etc.).
