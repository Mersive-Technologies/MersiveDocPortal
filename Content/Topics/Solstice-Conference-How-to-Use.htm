How to Use Solstice Conference

These instructions outline steps for meeting hosts to use the Mersive Solstice app to connect their video conference to Solstice content sharing and room peripherals, such as cameras and mics, plugged into the Solstice Pod.

Scheduled Conferences vs Unscheduled Conferences

Scheduled video conferences appear in advance on either a room calendar integrated with the Solstice Pod or on a Microsoft 365 calendar integrated in the Mersive Solstice app. The details for the conference – meeting ID, passcode, URL, etc. – must be included in the body of the scheduled event. Integrating a room or personal Microsoft 365 calendar, along with Solstice Pod configuration, enables faster starts for Solstice-connected video conferences. See Launch a Scheduled Video Conference for more details.

The Mersive Solstice app can also connect Solstice content sharing and Pod-connected room peripherals to a video conference not previously scheduled on an integrated calendar. In this scenario, the video conference needs to be started separately using the host's preferred conferencing service. See Connect to an Unscheduled Meeting in Solstice Conference for more details.

See Start a Video Conference for more details.

Using Solstice Conference with Dual Displays

If the Solstice Pod is connected to dual display monitors, the screen connected to HDMI 1 of the Pod will be available to be shared to the video conference. The screen connected to HDMI 2 will be visible to participants in the room but will not be shared as part of the conference. In the Mersive Solstice app layout screen, the display shared with the conference is the screen on the left.

 Mersive Solstice App Update Note

Older versions of the Mersive Solstice app for Windows and macOS provided a wide range of options for connecting to Solstice Conference and starting scheduled video conferences. Which video conference was started sometimes depended on opaque logic based on details of scheduled meetings in calendars integrated with the app and/or with the Pod.

App version 5.5.1 eliminates this confusion by only auto-launching video conference meetings that appear from the calendar integrated with the app. Mersive is looking into whether and how to best to add back the ability to auto-launch video conferences from room calendars integrated with a Solstice Pod. If this functionality is heavily used in your organization, please contact your Mersive representative – we would be interested to hear how it proved most useful to you to inform development going forward.
